What this is

My understanding, learning and philosophies on the education of pupils during their second developmental stage (11+). Using agile methodologies to produce collaborative value orientated education that brings learning to life for human beings who are considered children but are quickly encroaching upon adulthood.

Instead of trying to reform a system that doesn't work, I want to work with people who are just as aware of the failings of a broken system. Much as fixing and replacing broken parts on an old car will never make it a space shuttle, mending outdate forms of communication designed for a different age will never create engaged individuals


This is an "open source" resource with the ideas and philosophies that I have towards creating a "Good School" fit for the purpose of education rather than pigeon-holing young people into categories believing that the only purpose of schooling is vocational.
